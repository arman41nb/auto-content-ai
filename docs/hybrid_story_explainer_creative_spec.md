# Hybrid Story Explainer Creative Spec

Hybrid Story Explainer is the preferred production template for short-form educational Reels that need a mascot without becoming a mascot slideshow.

## Brand Style

- intelligent
- visual
- story-driven
- social-first
- clean
- premium
- not childish
- not PowerPoint
- not AI-trash
- not a generic slideshow

## Viewer Feeling

The viewer should feel:

- "Now I understand."
- "That was a clear example."
- "This is worth saving."
- "This is worth sending to someone."

## Default Structure

1. Hook with real-world context
2. Question from a proxy/person/business situation
3. Concrete setup/example
4. Mechanism explained visually
5. Real-world consequence
6. Contrast or different case
7. Nuance or twist
8. Takeaway

## Core Rule

Every scene must be a meaningful scene, not just a picture with a title.

## Mascot Role

Miko should be a guide, explainer, commentator, pointer, reaction character, and visual memory device.

Miko should not be the giant central subject in most scenes, decorative filler, a replacement for topic context, an unrelated toy in front of a random background, or stretched/cropped awkwardly.

Default mascot frame share target is 10-35 percent when Miko appears. More than 35 percent is risky and more than 45 percent fails unless the scene is explicitly justified as central.

## Proxy Characters

Allowed proxies:

- importer
- business owner
- student
- curious friend
- market watcher
- shipping manager
- shopper

The proxy asks or embodies the problem. Avoid close-up human hosts, fake celebrities, political figures, or real-person identity.

## Media Mix

Use the best visual for the scene, not the easiest visual.

- Real context scenes prefer Pexels, Unsplash, Wikimedia, or realistic AI when stock is weak.
- Mascot scenes use Miko as small contextual guide inside real topic context.
- Mechanism scenes use premium infographic reveals.
- If a visual is generic, stretched, cropped, unrelated, or title-card-like, it is not production-ready.

## Caption Style

Use `hybrid_editorial`.

- One compact caption block at a time.
- Normal caption box ratio should stay at or below 0.12.
- Hook/takeaway may reach 0.18.
- Captions must not cover Miko's face or the key object.
- The handle stays subtle.

## Economy Safety

Economy topics must avoid financial advice and trading recommendations. The relationship should be described as indirect or context-dependent when appropriate, and importers/exporters should be distinguished when relevant.
